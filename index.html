This website showcases projects developed for CPSC 581, focusing on human-computer interaction. Each selectable card under the Projects section outlines my design process, technical implementation, and evolving approach to interaction design.

The projects are presented in chronological order, reflecting improvements and changes in my individual design process over the course of the semester.

Select the "Family of Buttons" card to get started, then move through the remaining projects from left to right.
